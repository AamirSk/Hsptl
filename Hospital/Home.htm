Welcome

We are Kewal Endoscopy Center located in Jalgaon, Maharashtra, specializing in Endoscopies. We are licensed by the State of Maharashtra and the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid.

Our clinical staff is professionally trained to provide you with the highest quality of care. We pride ourselves on utilizing the latest in Medical technology.

Our center offers a comfortable environment with a highly trained medical staff that is focused on offering personalized patient care. We know exactly how many patients we will serve each day and staff appropriately to ensure each patient’s visit goes as quickly and smoothly as possible.

We invite you to take a look around our website to learn more about us, our services, how you can be prepared for surgery and more.
